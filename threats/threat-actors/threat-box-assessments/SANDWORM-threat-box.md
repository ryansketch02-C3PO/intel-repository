## Scoring Rationale

**Espionage — MEDIUM**
Sandworm does conduct intelligence collection, but their primary mandate is offensive operations — disruption and destruction — rather than quiet collection. Some espionage activity documented, but it is not their defining mission.

**Destructive — HIGH (9/10)**
NotPetya ($10B+ global damage), Ukraine power grid attacks (2015, 2016), Industroyer/Crashoverride — Sandworm is the most destructive cyber actor on earth by confirmed damage inflicted. Against US critical infrastructure and defense contractors, their ideology-driven mandate (4) and unparalleled destructive capability (5) with custom tooling (-0) produces a near-maximum score. The only reason it's not 5 on intent is that A&D is not their historical primary focus (energy/government is). In a conflict escalation scenario this becomes 5x5.

**Disruptive — 5x5 (10/10)**
Sandworm has taken down entire national power grids. Their pre-positioning on US critical infrastructure (documented by CISA) represents a latent disruptive capability that can be activated during a conflict. A&D manufacturing facilities depend on power grid stability — Sandworm disrupting US energy infrastructure disrupts A&D operations as a secondary effect, even without directly targeting the contractor.

**Supply Chain — HIGH (9/10)**
NotPetya was delivered via a compromised Ukrainian accounting software update — textbook supply chain attack. Sandworm has demonstrated the willingness and capability to weaponize software update mechanisms. Any A&D contractor with Ukrainian suppliers or shared software dependencies faces residual risk.

## A&D-Specific Risk Summary

Sandworm is Russia's most dangerous sabotage actor. They don't steal — they break things. For A&D, the primary risk is wartime/escalation scenario: Sandworm pre-positions on US energy infrastructure and activates those implants during a conflict, causing cascading disruption to manufacturing and operations. Secondary risk: supply chain destructive payload delivery (NotPetya model). If US-Russia relations deteriorate further, Sandworm's threat level to all US critical infrastructure including A&D jumps immediately.
